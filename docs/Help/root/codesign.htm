Authenticode Code Signing

You can optionally use Microsoft's SignCode.exe utility to digitally sign the self-extracting archives FreeExtractor creates. This helps assure end-users of its integrity and better guarentees its authenticity. This is ideally suited for large-scale public software and document distribution.

SignCode.exe can be found in Microsoft's Authenticode Package. In addition, you must have a valid digital certificate issued by a third party (like Verisign or Thawte).
