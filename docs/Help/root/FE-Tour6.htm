Step #5: Custom Icons

FreeExtractor allows you to optionally modify the icon of the self-extractor you create. You may choose any 32x32 pixel, 256-color icon. (The icon must be exactly 2,238 bytes in size.) For your convienience, FreeExtractor comes with a collection of icons for this purpose. They are installed in your FreeExtractor directory. (By default, c:\program files\FreeExtractor\Icons\)

If you do not select an icon, a default one will be provided for you.
